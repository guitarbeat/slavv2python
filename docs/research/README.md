# Research

Cited research syntheses that inform the project's direction — distinct from:

- **[plans/](../plans/)** — scoped specs for active initiatives
- **[brainstorms/](../brainstorms/)** — raw pre-plan ideas (deleted once promoted)
- **[solutions/](../solutions/)** — solved problems and runbooks
- **[investigations/](../investigations/)** — archival history of past decisions

A research doc here gathers external evidence (with sources and confidence) on a
question that shapes a roadmap phase. It is durable reference material; promote
concrete actions from it into [plans/](../plans/) or [TODO.md](../TODO.md).
